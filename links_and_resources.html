Swissbeatbox The YouTube channel for beatboxing. The videos from many major tournaments are hosted on this channel, as well as many beatboxer solo features. Even when beatboxers have their own channel, their performances will often be hosted on Swissbeatbox. This is why "shoutout to Swissbeatbox and my friend Pepouni" is a thing.
